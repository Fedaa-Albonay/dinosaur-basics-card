Dinosaur

Dinosaurs are a diverse group of reptiles belonging to the clade Dinosauria that first appeared between 243 and 233 million years ago during the Triassic period. They were the dominant terrestrial vertebrates for over 160 million years throughout the Jurassic and Cretaceous periods

  • Scientific Name: Dinosaurs
  • Length: 39.7 meters (130 feet) and heights of 18 m (59 ft)

Using one of the above definitions, dinosaurs can be generally described as archosaurs with hind limbs held erect beneath the body.
